AeroVJTI – Connecting, inspiring and activating aviation enthusiasts

Making planes requires quite a few forms of resources - time, energy, material and so on. But the one that will eventually help translate the others into a successful aircraft is money. From purchasing competition grade electronics for the aircraft, to traveling in one, funds are required for material procurement, shipping and transportation.
AeroVJTI seeks your assistance in generating this vital resource. Given the outreach AeroVJTI has, rest assured, the association formed will mutually benefit both of us and will not be traffic on a one-way street.
