The Society

AeroVJTI is the aero-modelling club of the Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute. A completely student run society, AeroVJTI promotes aero-modelling and design. We design and build radio controlled Aircrafts and participate in various national and international competitions like American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA) Design/Build/Fly held at USA every year, and nationwide competition SAE India. Besides these, we have also competed in the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) Aero-Design in USA and Future Flight Design (FFD) held in Turkey.

We mentor the young and budding juniors to develop their own designs and models through various workshops conducted in schools, colleges. The club also conducts workshops and awareness program in various NGOs under 'PRATIGYA' to spread knowledge and interest.

Our vision is to represent India internationally and earn a name for India, the club and also bring attention to the lesser explored field in our country. Combining our knowledge, experience and ideas we strive to reach the top.
